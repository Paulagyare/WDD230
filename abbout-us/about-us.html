How We Started

Established in 2007 but practiced since 2004, our team of skilled professionals has been working to proudly serve residents of the GTA. Located in the heart of Mississauga at 58 Dundas Street West, our store has provided excellent service in the art of fruit arrangements. We only use the finest fruits available and our orders are always freshly made. We use a variety of fresh fruits in our arrangements that promote a healthy lifestyle. Some of these include cantaloupes, pineapples, grapes, watermelon, honeydew, oranges, kiwi, strawberries and plums. We also add in any vegetables or other fruits that are requested, upon availability. For a small additional fee, chocolate strawberries, a teddy bear or balloon can be added to the basket. Delivery options are also available. Order your fruit basket today – A healthy gift for someone special! Our Guarantee – Our arrangements are true to size and appearance. Compared to leading competitors we offer considerably lower prices for superior quality and service. You have our guarantee of satisfaction.

The Area We Operate In

Carlsbad is a coastal city located in the North County region of San Diego County, California. It is a popular tourist destination due to its beautiful beaches, year-round mild climate, and a variety of recreational activities. Carlsbad is known for its charming village atmosphere and is home to a number of art galleries, boutique shops, and restaurants. Visitors can enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking and biking in the nearby hills or kayaking and paddleboarding in the lagoons.
